## Context

The platform will evolve over time and architectural decisions may be revisited.
Without documentation, future contributors cannot understand the reasoning behind key technical choices.

## Decision

The project will will use Architecture Decision Records (ADRs).
All significant architectural decisions must be documented before implementation.

## Consequences

### Positive

- Historical record of decisions
- Easier onboarding
- Improved technical discussions
- Clear tradeoff documentation

### Negative

- Additional documentation effort
